## Scoop question (verbatim target)

Is there a **published certified three-way triage** — auto-pass / auto-reject / **defer** —
on **MVTec AD (or VisA)** image anomaly detection, with **coupled escaped-defect AND
false-reject** conformal guarantees and **per-category (Mondrian) certifiability floors**?

(below); a miss confirms C1 stands.

## Verdict

**CLEAR — no scoop.** No published work delivers a certified three-way (auto-pass/auto-reject/
defer) triage on MVTec AD or VisA with coupled escaped-defect and false-reject conformal
guarantees and per-category certifiability floors. The closest published systems remain the
three already cited (Kumar et al., CRC-SGAD, Shen & Liu); each differs on a load-bearing axis
(substrate, estimand coupling, or the defer option). The two new-since-2026-07-11 candidates
(Yu & Liu selective-risk certificate; the RESS cyber-physical CPS paper) are adjacent but
disjoint, and the previously-flagged CPS item is confirmed non-scooping.

**Pre-decided pivot (NOT triggered).** Had a scoop appeared, the design's pivot is: promote
C2 (the audit) + C3 (the tool) to primary and demote C1 to replication-with-floors — the
paper is built so C2/C3 stand without C1's novelty. No pivot needed this scan.
